Cool Roof Tiles for a Smarter, Cooler Home

Cool roof tiles are engineered to solve one of India's most pressing building challenges: heat gain through the roof. By reflecting solar radiation and emitting absorbed heat rapidly, these tiles can reduce roof surface temperatures by up to 40°C and cut air conditioning energy consumption significantly.

Our collection spans high-SRI white porcelain for maximum performance, terracotta classics with modern nano-coatings, and large-format concrete-look slabs ideal for accessible rooftops. Every product is rated for Solar Reflectance Index and Thermal Emittance — the two metrics that matter most for certified cool-roof compliance under LEED, GRIHA, and BEE standards.

1
Roof Deck Preparation

Ensure the roof deck is clean, dry, and structurally sound. Repair any cracks or damaged sections and allow concrete to cure fully before tiling.

2
Waterproofing Layer

Apply an approved waterproof membrane across the full roof surface, with upturns at parapets and penetrations. Allow full cure before proceeding.

3
Dry Layout & Slope Check

Dry-lay tiles to verify drainage falls (minimum 1:80 slope recommended). Mark reference lines from the centre outward for symmetrical placement.

4
Adhesive & Setting

Use a flexible, weather-resistant tile adhesive rated for external use. Apply with a notched trowel and back-butter large-format tiles. Press firmly to eliminate voids.

5
Grouting & Expansion Joints

Use a UV-stable, weatherproof grout. Provide expansion joints at 3–4 m intervals and at perimeter edges to accommodate thermal movement. Seal joints with a flexible sealant.
