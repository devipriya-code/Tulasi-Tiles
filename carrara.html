About This Tile

Carrara Elegance —
Luxury Redefined

Inspired by the legendary white-and-grey veining of genuine Carrara marble from the quarries of Tuscany, our Carrara Elegance tile series brings an ageless Italian aesthetic into the heart of Indian homes.

Each tile is precision-engineered using high-definition inkjet printing on a dense vitrified porcelain body — faithfully reproducing the natural depth, tonal variation, and delicate veining of real marble, without any of the maintenance demands that natural stone requires.

Whether you are designing a grand open-plan living room or an intimate family lounge, Carrara Elegance sets the tone with effortless sophistication. Its polished finish reflects light beautifully, making every space feel larger, brighter, and unmistakably refined.
