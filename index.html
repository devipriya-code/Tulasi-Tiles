Company Profile

About Tulasi Tiles

Established in 2016, Tulasi Tiles is a leading Tiles Supplier in Tirupur, known for delivering premium-quality tiles that enhance both residential and commercial spaces.

As a trusted Wholesaler, Supplier, and Trader, we deal in Vitrified Tiles, Ceramic Wall Tiles, Marble Floor Tiles and Glazed Vitrified Tiles (GVT), offering a wide variety of designs, finishes, and sizes.

With a strong understanding of current market trends, we continuously update our collections to provide modern designs, elegant finishes, and reliable materials that meet the diverse needs of our customers.

  • Specially Designed Tiles for Walls and Floors
  • Wide Range for Homes, Villas, Offices & Commercial Spaces
  • Carefully Selected & Quality-Checked Products
  • 100% Customer Satisfaction is Our Priority
