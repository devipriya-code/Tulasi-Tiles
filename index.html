About Tulasi Tiles

Established in 2016, Tulasi Tiles is a leading Tiles Supplier in Tirupur, known for delivering premium-quality tiles that enhance both residential and commercial spaces.

As a trusted Wholesaler, Supplier, and Trader, we deal in Vitrified Tiles, Ceramic Wall Tiles, Marble Floor Tiles and Glazed Vitrified Tiles (GVT), offering a wide variety of designs, finishes, and sizes.

With a strong understanding of current market trends, we continuously update our collections to provide modern designs, elegant finishes, and reliable materials that meet the diverse needs of our customers. Our commitment to timely delivery ,competitive pricing , and personalized service has helped us build long-term relationships with clients across Tirupur and surrounding regions.
