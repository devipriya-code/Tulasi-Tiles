About Tulasi Tiles

With years of expertise in the tile industry, Tulasi Tiles is committed to enhancing homes and commercial spaces with superior quality tiles. We blend durability, elegant design, and modern innovation to deliver flooring and wall solutions that stand the test of time.

Our Story

Established in 2016, Tulasi Tiles has grown into a trusted wholesaler, supplier, and trader of high-quality tiles in Tirupur. From the very beginning, our focus has been on delivering reliable products that combine durability, style, and value.

We offer a wide range of premium tiles including Full Body Double Charge Vitrified Tiles, Vitrified Floor Tiles, Ceramic Wall Tiles, GVT Glazed Vitrified Tiles, and Marble Floor Tiles, catering to both residential and commercial needs.

Today, Tulasi Tiles is recognized as one of the most trusted choices for bathroom tiles and floor tiles in Tirupur. With a variety of modern designs, textures, and finishes, we help homeowners, architects, and builders create spaces that are elegant, functional, and long-lasting.
